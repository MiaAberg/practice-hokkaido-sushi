Welcome to Hokkaido Sushi

So glad you found us! We opened the doors to our brand new restaurants in Stockholm and Uppsala in the year of 2021 and we are so excited to introduce the authentic taste of Hokkaido sushi from Japan here in Sweden. All the ingrediens in our dishes are carefully chosen and fair trade. You can feel 100% sure to enjoy real sushi at its best at Hokkaido Sushi. Hope to serve you soon!
